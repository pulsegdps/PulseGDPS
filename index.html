How to join PulseGDPS

To join, first make sure you have a copy of Geometry Dash. Once you do that, there are 5 ways of joining PulseGDPS,
and the recommended ways are with the installers, but those do not have a tutorial (because its stupid simple).
There is also an android download but I don't use android so I wouldn't know how to install an APK file.

Method 1Windows only
Download the zip file, then extract the contents into any folder. Then you may open the folder you extracted the contents into and run "pulse.exe".

Method 2using mods, any platform
Make sure Geometry Dash is installed on your computer, then download and install Geode from: https://geode-sdk.org/.
Then open Geometry Dash and click the pentagon at the bottom right-ish. Then click "Download" at the top,
and search for the "GDPS Switcher" mod and press "Get", then click "Install" at the bottom left and wait for it to do its thing,
then click "Restart Now". Once you did all of that, you can click the servers icon at the bottom right-ish.
Then click "New" on the bottom right. Choose a server name, then in the "Server URL" box, type https://puls.ps.fhgdps.com.
You may then click "Use", then the back arrow on the top left, and finally press "Restart now".
